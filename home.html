Methi Carrot Rice

A great one-pot meal to use your leftover rice for a lazy summer Sunday! Chop some carrots, beans and fresh methi leaves. Heat a pan with some oil and add sesame seeds, cinnamon, black pepper, and green chillis. Then add the vegetables and the rice. Mix them well together and have it fresh!
