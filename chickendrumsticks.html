Crunchy Chicken Twisties drumsticks

Crunchy Chicken Twisties drumsticks are a delicious and innovative take on traditional chicken drumsticks. They are typically made by marinating chicken drumsticks in a flavorful seasoning blend, then coating them with a crunchy mixture made from crushed twisties (a type of crunchy corn snack), breadcrumbs, and spices. The drumsticks are then baked or fried until they are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. The twisties coating adds a unique texture and flavor profile to the chicken, creating a tasty and satisfying dish that is sure to be a hit with both kids and adults alike. These drumsticks are perfect for serving as a main course at dinner or as a fun appetizer for parties and gatherings. Ingredients

  • 100g chicken Twisties
  • 3/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 cup plain flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 can olive oil spray
  • 1 tbs fresh thyme leaves chopped
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper *to taste

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Place Twisties in a food processor. Process until chopped finely, resembling coarse breadcrumbs. Place panko breadcrumbs on a plate, then add crushed Twisties, garlic powder and paprika and mix to combine.
  2. Place flour on a plate. Season with salt and pepper. Whisk egg and milk in a shallow bowl.
  3. Toss 1 piece of chicken in flour to coat. Dip in the egg mixture, then toss in breadcrumbs, pressing to secure. Place onto a plate. Repeat with remaining chicken. Refrigerate for 10 minutes.
  4. Preheat the oven to 200C/180C fan-forced. Line an oven tray with baking paper.
  5. Arrange chicken in a single layer on prepared tray. Spray all over with oil spray. Bake, turning once, for 45 minutes or until golden and chicken is cooked through. Serve chicken sprinkled with thyme leaves.
