Aloo Parantha

Traditionally, the breads are cooked on a flat Indian griddle pan (but any frying pan will do) and are slathered with creamy butter as they cook. The parantha then puff up like little pillows and then all you need to do is serve them quickly, ideally with spicy mango pickle, fresh yoghurt or a big blob of even more butter.
