Tandoori Chicken Tikka Salad

Tandoori Chicken Tikka Salad is a delicious fusion dish that combines the flavors of traditional Indian tandoori chicken tikka with a refreshing salad. It typically features grilled or roasted chicken tikka, marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, served on a bed of fresh salad greens and vegetables. To prepare Tandoori Chicken Tikka Salad, boneless chicken pieces are marinated in a blend of yogurt, ginger garlic paste, lemon juice, and a mixture of spices such as garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, and sometimes kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves). The marinated chicken is then skewered and grilled in a tandoor oven or baked in a regular oven until cooked through and charred on the edges, imparting a smoky flavor. Once the chicken is cooked, it is typically sliced or cubed and arranged on a bed of mixed salad greens such as lettuce, spinach, or arugula. Additional vegetables like cucumber, tomatoes, onions, bell peppers, and carrots may be added for crunch and freshness. The salad is often garnished with fresh herbs like cilantro or mint and drizzled with a dressing made from yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Some variations may include a spicy mint chutney or a tangy tamarind sauce for added flavor. Tandoori Chicken Tikka Salad offers a perfect balance of flavors and textures, with the smoky and spicy chicken complementing the crispness of the salad greens and the coolness of the yogurt dressing. It's a satisfying and healthy dish that can be enjoyed as a light meal or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Yogurt (Greek yogurt works well), Tandoori masala powder
  • Lemon juice, Garlic, minced, Ginger, grated
  • Salt, to taste
  • Mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula, etc.)
  • Cucumber, sliced, Tomatoes, diced, Red onion, thinly sliced, Bell peppers (red, green, yellow), sliced
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Olive oil
  • Optional: Yogurt or mayonnaise for creaminess

Step by Step Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, tandoori masala powder, lemon juice, minced garlic, grated ginger, and salt. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers or grill them directly on the grill grate.
  4. Grill the chicken for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and have charred edges. Remove from the grill and set aside.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, sliced cucumber, diced tomatoes, thinly sliced red onion, and sliced bell peppers. Toss the salad ingredients together to mix well.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.
  7. Arrange the grilled tandoori chicken tikka pieces on top of the prepared salad. Drizzle the dressing over the salad.
  8. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  9. Serve the Tandoori Chicken Tikka Salad immediately, accompanied by lemon wedges on the side for squeezing over the salad.
