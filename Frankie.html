Ingredients

    FOR DOUGH:
  • 2 cup maida
  • ½ cup wheat flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ cup curd
  • water, for kneading oil, for roasting
FOR STUFFING:
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • pinch hing
  • 2 chilli, finely chopped, 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp ginger paste, ½ tsp turmeric 1 tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp coriander powder, ½ tsp cumin powder, ½ tsp aamchur
  • 2 tbsp chilli sauce, 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 3 potato, boiled & mashed
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp lemon juice

    • Step by Step Instructions

    1. Firstly, in a large bowl take 2 cup maida, ½ cup wheat flour, ½ tsp salt and ¼ cup curd.
    2. Mix well making sure everything is well combined.
    3. Now add water as required and knead to smooth and soft dough.
    4. Grease the dough with oil, cover and rest for 20 minutes.
    5. After the dough has rested well, knead the dough again and pinch a ball-sized dough.
    6. Dust with maida and roll thin.
    7. Now cook the roti on hot pan spreading oil.
    8. Flip over and cook both sides.
    9. Also, spread the oil and cook until the roti is cooked from both sides. keep aside.
    10. Take roti and spread green chutney.
    11. Place the prepared aloo tikki, and top with coriander, chaat masala, onion, chilli vinegar and tomato sauce.
    12. Roll tight and wrap in butter paper.
    13. Finally, veg frankie recipe is ready to enjoy with tomato sauce.
