Beetroot Roti

Ingredients

  • 1 medium sized beet root
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • ½ tsp jeera powder
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala powder
  • ½ tsp carom seeds
  • 2 tsp oil
  • Salt as per the taste
  • Oil for frying

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Peel and grate the beetroot and transfer to a mixing bowl.
  2. Add salt, chilli powder, jeera powder, garam masala, carom seeds and mix well. Keep aside for 10 mins.
  3. Now add the whole wheat flour to the mixture. Using hands, knead to the soft dough while squeezing the beetroot so as to use the water in the beetroot for mixing. Sprinkle little water while kneading the dough.
  4. Heat a griddle/tawa. Roll the rotis using a rolling pin and roast them on both sides with ghee or oil.
