Always we prefer mirchi bajji. In this mirchi bajji we do 2 stuffings, 1 with tamarind and Ajwain masala and 2 with onion masala mixture.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup tamarind pulp
  • 1/2 cup Roasted Chana dal powder
  • 2 tsp Ajwain seeds (crushed)
  • 1 cup Besan powder
  • 2 tsp Rice flour
  • 1 tsp Hot oil
  • 1 tsp Ajwain seeds
  • 1 tsp Red chilli powder, 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 pinch Cooking soda
  • 1 cup cup(finely chopped) - Onion
  • 1/4 cup cup(finely chopped) - Coriander leaves
  • 3-4 tsp Lemon juice

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Soak tamarind in water.
  2. Clean chilli and tap with dry cloth. Slit in the middle and remove seeds. Prepare stuffing 1- take a bowl extract tamarind pulp(thick) Add slightly crushed ajwain seeds, salt, roasted Chana dal powder mix well. Now stuff the above into mirchi using spoon and keep it aside.
  3. Take a pan add oil to deep fry and heat it on low flames
  4. Prepare batter - Take a bowl add all ingredients mentioned under For batter mix well, at last add above 1 tsp hot oil and mix well.
  5. Drop pinch of batter in oil if oil is hot, batter will float up immediately.
  6. Now dip stuffed mirchi in batter and coat it well in batter and drop it into hot oil.
  7. Fry mirchi bajji fry on both sides till it changes colour and take it out into the plate. Repeat the process removing.
  8. Switch off the flame and add ground nuts fry till changes colour and take it into plate. You can also use fried groundnuts too.
  9. For stuffing 2- Take a bowl add all ingredients mentioned under For stuffing 2 one after other and mix everything well.
  10. Now slit fried stuffed mirchi bajji in middle with knife and stuff above onion masala stuff using spoon.
  11. That's it yummy and tasty stuffed mirchi bajji is ready. Trust me this is the best evening snack to must have in this winter season.
