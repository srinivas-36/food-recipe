Thai Noodle Salad

Thai Noodle Salad is a vibrant and refreshing dish that combines a variety of flavors and textures. It typically features cooked rice noodles, mixed with a colorful array of fresh vegetables, herbs, and a tangy dressing inspired by Thai cuisine. The salad often includes ingredients such as shredded carrots, cucumber, bell peppers, bean sprouts, and thinly sliced cabbage, along with fragrant herbs like cilantro, mint, and Thai basil. Sometimes, protein such as grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu may be added to make the salad more substantial. The dressing for Thai Noodle Salad is usually a combination of ingredients like lime juice, fish sauce, soy sauce, garlic, chili, and a touch of sugar, creating a perfect balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy flavors. This salad is served chilled or at room temperature, making it ideal for hot weather or as a refreshing side dish. It's a versatile dish that can be customized to suit individual preferences and dietary restrictions, making it a popular choice for gatherings and potlucks.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cabbage shredded
  • 1 bunch baby bok choy shredded
  • 4 spring onions finely chopped
  • 100 g toasted slivered almonds
  • 100 g Chang's fried noodles
  • 1 carrot julienned (optional)
  • 1 red capsicum julienned (optional)

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup caster sugar
  • 1 tbs soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup light olive oil
  • 2 tsp sesame oil (to taste)

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Dressing: Combine all dressing ingredients in a jar with a tight lid, and shake until sugar has dissolved.
  2. Salad: Toss together cabbage, bok choy and spring onions in a large salad bowl.
  3. Just before serving, add dressing and toss, then add almonds and noodles, and toss thoroughly to combine.
