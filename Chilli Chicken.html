Andhra Chilli Chicken

Andhra Chilli Chicken is a spicy and flavorful dish from the Andhra Pradesh region of India. It is made by marinating chicken pieces in a blend of spices and then frying them until crispy. Ingredients

  • 500 gram : Chicken, Cut Into Pieces
  • 2 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tablespoon Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  • As required Salt To Taste
  • as per your need Oil For Frying
  • as required Curry Leaves
  • 2 Green Chilies, Slit
  • 1 Onion, Chopped
  • as required Coriander Leaves For Garnish

Step by Step Instructions

  1. In a bowl, marinate chicken pieces with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the marinated chicken until golden brown and crispy.
  3. In another pan, heat oil and add curry leaves, green chilies, and chopped onions. Saute until onions turn translucent.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add fried chicken pieces and toss well to coat them with the masala.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
