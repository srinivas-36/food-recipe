Coconut And Jaggery Modaks

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 3 tsp ghee (divided usage)
  • 1 cup rice flour
  • Pinch of salt (optional)
    • For the filling:
  • 2 cups grated coconut
  • 1 cup powdered or grated jaggery
  • 1 tsp cardamom powder

Step by Step Instructions

  1. To prepare the filling (puran), heat a pan and add the grated jaggery and coconut, and saut√© until well mixed and thickened. Mix in the cardamom powder and turn off the flame. Allow the mixture to cool down.
  2. To prepare the dough, bring the water to a boil. Add a little ghee and salt and mix well. Turn off the flame and add the rice flour, stirring to combine well. Cover and let it rest for four to five minutes until slightly cooled. Transfer the dough to a clean plate, add one to two teaspoons of ghee, and knead to a smooth dough.
  3. Divide the dough into lemon-sized balls and keep them covered.
  4. Divide the prepared filling into equal one tablespoon portions and form neat balls, the same number as the rice flour dough balls. Set aside.
  5. To make the modaks, take a lemon-sized ball of rice flour dough and flatten it. Use your fingers and thumb to make a small cup out of the dough. Place one ball of the sweet filling in the centre and bring the edges to the top, sealing it. Ensure the top is pointed for the modak shape.
  6. Repeat with the rest of the dough and filling.
  7. Grease the steamer with ghee and place all the prepared modaks on it. Cover and steam for 10 to 12 minutes or until the outer cover becomes shiny.
  8. Remove from the steamer, set aside to cool, and serve.
