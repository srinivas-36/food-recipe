Moroccan Lamb Burgers

Moroccan Lamb Burgers are a flavorful twist on the classic burger, inspired by the rich and aromatic cuisine of Morocco. These burgers are typically made by combining ground lamb with a variety of spices and herbs, such as cumin, coriander, cinnamon, paprika, and mint, to create a fragrant and savory flavor profile. The ground lamb mixture is formed into patties and then grilled, pan-seared, or baked until cooked through and juicy. These burgers are often served on toasted buns or flatbreads and topped with a variety of accompaniments such as lettuce, tomato, cucumber, red onion, and a tangy yogurt or tzatziki sauce.The combination of tender lamb, aromatic spices, and fresh toppings makes Moroccan Lamb Burgers a delicious and satisfying meal option that is perfect for any occasion. They offer a unique and exotic twist on the traditional burger experience, adding a touch of North African flair to your dining table.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg lamb mince
  • 2 tbs Moroccan seasoning
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbs lemon rind zested
  • 2 tbs tomato paste
  • 2 tbs honey
  • 1/4 cup dates finely chopped
  • 1 onion diced small
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup fresh breadcrumbs

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients. Refrigerate, covered, for approximately 2 hours
  2. Shape into burger patties and fry on medium heat until cooked through, turning once.
  3. Arrange patties onto bread rolls, add lettuce and sauce of choice.
